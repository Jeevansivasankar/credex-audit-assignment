## Lead Value
A converted lead for Credex is highly valuable. If an average Series A startup has a $5,000/mo AI spend, and Credex can source credits at a 20% discount ($1,000/mo savings), Credex might take a 30% cut of the savings as their revenue model.
- Revenue per customer: $300/mo ($3,600 ARR).
- LTV (assuming 3-year retention): ~$10,800.

## CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- **Cold Email/Outbound:** Cost of Clay/Apollo subscription ($100/mo). If we get 10 leads, CAC is $10.
- **Hacker News / Organic:** $0 direct cost, just time. CAC is effectively $0.
- **Shareable Links (Viral):** $0.

## Conversion Funnel to Profitability
To make this tool a sustainable growth engine:
- **Traffic to Audit:** 20%
- **Audit to Email Capture (Lead):** 15% (3% of total traffic)
- **Email Lead to Consultation Booked:** 10% (0.3% of total traffic)
- **Consultation to Credit Purchase:** 25% (0.075% of total traffic)

If we want 1 net new customer (LTV $10,800), we need:
- 4 Consultations
- 40 Email Leads
- 266 Audits
- 1,333 Website Visitors
With an organic CAC of near $0, this funnel is incredibly profitable.

## Path to $1M ARR
To reach $1,000,000 ARR, Credex needs roughly 277 customers (at $3,600 ARR each).
Using the funnel above, over 18 months, we need:
- 277 customers
- 1,108 Consultations
- 11,080 Leads
- 369,000 Website Visitors (~20k visitors per month).
For this to be true, the tool must become a standard utility linked in startup playbooks and consistently shared via the viral URL loop. We would need to expand the audit tool to support AWS/GCP AI endpoints to capture larger enterprise spend.
